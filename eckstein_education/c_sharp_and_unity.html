ArtWords

×

Art words is a program that combines a child’s love of coloring objects with vocabulary and reading practice.

The teacher will first type in twelve vocabulary words to practice. These will randomly be spread throughout the game board. Then an image will be chosen.

Initially white, the students come to the screen one-by-one to touch an image. Color is added, and students read the words that appear (or say the color if no words were entered).

Shape Finder

×

Combining the love children have for interactive activities with shape recognition and recall, this game allows teachers to select from one to twelve unique shapes (circle, rectangle, trapezoid, etc). These will be “hidden” behind art scenes.

When students touch the art, for example the horse in the farm scene, the art will disappear and reveal the colored shape.

Paint Bucket

×

Practicing colors has never been easier.

Choose the colors to include in the random selection, and each time the pour button is pressed, colors will be poured into the circle.

Students get to interact and shout out the colors they see!

Patterns Never End

×

Practicing pattern recognition and prediction has never been easier.

This program was built to follow the chapters in a specific book, but it includes vocabulary words as well as sound patterns, shape patterns, color patterns, and image patterns.

From simple ABAB to ABCDABCD. You can practice it all!

Hot Potato

×

This program is little more than musical and visual feedback for the children’s game of “Hot Potato.”

There are two modes: automatic and manual. In automatic, the timer will play the music for up to 15 seconds before stopping long enough for students to read a vocabulary word on a card, or perform an action based on teacher’s requirements.

In manual mode, the teacher controls when the music will start and stop.

Master Mind

×

Can you guess the secret combination in just 10 tries?
