About Me

Randall Eckstein

My name is Randall. I am originally from Utah, though I have spent the last twenty years in China teaching English.

It was in China that I met my wife. We were married in 2012, and my son was born in 2014.

When the pandemic hit, I decided to use the time to learn computer programming. I had designed web pages in the late nineties, back when everything was hard coded and there was no quick-design software.

My passion is not actually designing web pages. I love the backend. I am proficient in C# and Python, and I've dipped my toe into Java. I'm pursuing a degree in web design and development, because it was the closest degree to software development I could see.
